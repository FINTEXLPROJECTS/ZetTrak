How It Works

Get Started with ZetTrak in Minutes

Follow these simple steps to set up your company, manage employees, track attendance, and process payroll — all in one place.

Start Free Now
1

Sign Up & Register Your Company

Click Sign Up Free on the home page. Enter your company name, email, and password. ZetTrak creates a dedicated workspace for your organisation — isolated and secure.

Company Registration Admin Account Instant Setup
2

Add Your Employees

From the Admin Dashboard, go to Employees → Add Employee. Fill in their name, department, designation, and shift. Each employee gets their own login to access their personal dashboard, payslips, and leave requests.

Individual Profiles Departments & Roles Bulk Import
3

Track Attendance Daily

Employees check in and out from their dashboard. Admins can view the full attendance list, edit records, approve regularization requests, and see who is present, absent, or on leave — all in real time.

Check In / Out Regularization Calendar View
4

Manage Leaves

Employees apply for leaves directly from their dashboard. Admins receive instant notifications and can approve or reject with one click. Leave balances update automatically and sync with payroll.

Apply & Approve Leave Balances Leave Types
5

Process Payroll

Navigate to Payroll in the admin panel. ZetTrak automatically calculates gross pay, deductions, and net salary based on attendance and leave data. Generate payslips that employees can download anytime.

Auto Calculation Payslip Download Deductions & Bonuses
6

Generate Reports & Send Announcements

Use the Reports section to export attendance, payroll, leave, and department summaries. Keep your team informed with the Announcements feature — broadcast messages to all employees instantly.

Export Reports Announcements Notifications
